How to use these flashcards

Each card shows an English word or phrase on the front. Tap or click to flip it and see the definition and an example sentence. Mark cards as "Got it" to track your progress — the progress bar fills as you work through the deck.

Use Shuffle to randomize card order. If you finish a deck, you can restart it or focus only on the cards you missed. Your progress is saved automatically in your browser.

Tips for vocabulary retention

Research on spaced repetition shows that reviewing material at increasing intervals — minutes, hours, days — dramatically improves long-term retention. Don't try to learn every word in one session. Study a deck once, then return to it after a day, then a week. Focus on the cards you missed.
