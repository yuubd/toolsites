About G9 Toolkit

Free tools, built for everyone.

What Is G9 Toolkit?

G9 Toolkit is a growing collection of free, easy-to-use online tools designed to help you get things done faster. Whether you're calculating a mortgage, converting a file, or checking your word count, our tools are built to be instant, accurate, and accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

We believe useful tools should be free and simple. That's why every tool on G9 Toolkit:

  • Runs entirely in your browser — no data is sent to our servers
  • Requires no account, login, or signup
  • Works on any device — desktop, tablet, or mobile
  • Loads fast and works offline once cached

Privacy First

Your data never leaves your browser. All calculations and conversions happen locally on your device. We don't store, track, or sell your inputs.

Disclaimer
